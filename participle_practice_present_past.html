Section B: Present Participle with "en"

Instructions: Translate these sentences into French using "en + present participle".

6. By studying hard, I passed the exam.
Example: En étudiant dur, j'ai réussi l'examen.
7. Through working together, we finished on time.
8. Being tired, she went to bed early.

Section C: Passé Composé with AVOIR

Instructions: Complete these sentences with the correct passé composé form (use AVOIR).

11. Tu __________ (vendre) ta voiture?

Section E: AVOIR or ÊTRE? (Tricky cases)

Instructions: Some verbs use AVOIR when they have a direct object. Choose correctly!

21. Je __________ (monter) les valises. (I brought up the suitcases)
22. Je __________ (monter) par l'escalier. (I went up the stairs)
23. Il __________ (sortir) la poubelle. (He took out the trash)
24. Nous (feminine) __________ (sortir) hier soir. (We went out last night)
